General Knowledge Rabbits

Rabbit - Holland Lop

Holland Lops are the smallest lop breed, weighing in at 4 pounds or less. Occasionally, we will have pet rabbits for sale that exceed 4 pounds, but they will definitely stay under 4.5 pounds which is still very small. A common health problem in Holland Lops is their teeth. Since they have flat faces, they are prone to getting a condition known as malocclusion, which misaligned teeth.

Malabar Giant Squrriel

Malabar giant squirrel is one of the most cute and lovable squirrels around the globe. This animal exhibits 2 - 3 types of color pattern with shades of black, brown and deep red.
